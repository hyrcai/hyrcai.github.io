    Intro

    I'm a designer with the heart of an artist and an appreciation for code. From CA, currently based in PA. I'm very detail-oriented and I love creating and refining visuals of all kinds. I enjoy wearing many hats and am most happy when I can work with other designers on multiple aspects of design at once. Currently working at NOVID as a motion, graphic, and product designer.
